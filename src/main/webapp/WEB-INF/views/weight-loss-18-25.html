Weight Loss Plan (18-25)

Diet Plan

  • Breakfast: Oatmeal with berries and a handful of almonds.
  • Lunch: Grilled chicken salad with mixed greens, cucumbers, and olive oil dressing.
  • Snacks: A piece of fruit and a handful of walnuts.
  • Dinner: Stir-fried vegetables with tofu and brown rice.

Gym Routine

  • Cardio workouts (HIIT, running, or cycling) 4-5 times a week.
  • Strength training 2-3 days a week focusing on bodyweight exercises.
  • Core strengthening (planks, leg raises, Russian twists).
  • Stretching or yoga for flexibility and recovery.
