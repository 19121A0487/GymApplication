Weight Loss Plan (25-40)

Diet Plan

  • Breakfast: Scrambled eggs with spinach and a slice of whole-grain toast.
  • Lunch: Quinoa bowl with grilled salmon, avocado, and roasted vegetables.
  • Snacks: Carrot sticks with hummus or a protein shake.
  • Dinner: Grilled chicken breast with a side of steamed broccoli and sweet potatoes.

Gym Routine

  • Combination of cardio and strength training 4-5 days a week.
  • High-intensity interval training (HIIT) 2-3 times a week.
  • Weight lifting exercises targeting all major muscle groups.
  • Focus on stretching and mobility exercises for injury prevention.
