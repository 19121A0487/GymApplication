Weight Gain Plan (18-25)

Diet Plan

  • Breakfast: 3 scrambled eggs, whole grain toast with avocado, and a glass of whole milk.
  • Lunch: Grilled chicken sandwich with cheese, spinach, and sweet potatoes.
  • Snacks: Protein shake with peanut butter, banana, and oats.
  • Dinner: Baked salmon, quinoa, and steamed broccoli.

Gym Routine

  • Strength training (heavy lifting) 4-5 days a week focusing on compound movements (squats, deadlifts, bench press).
  • Increase calorie intake by incorporating protein-rich meals and snacks.
  • Ensure proper recovery through stretching and rest days.
  • Core strengthening exercises to build a solid foundation.
