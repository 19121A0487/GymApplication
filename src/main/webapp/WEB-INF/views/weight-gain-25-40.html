Weight Gain Plan (25-40)

Diet Plan

  • Breakfast: 2 boiled eggs, oatmeal with nuts and berries, and a glass of orange juice.
  • Lunch: Chicken stir-fry with mixed vegetables, brown rice, and olive oil dressing.
  • Snacks: Greek yogurt with granola and a protein bar.
  • Dinner: Steak with mashed potatoes and steamed green beans.

Gym Routine

  • Strength training 4-5 days a week focusing on muscle-building exercises (bench press, squats, rows).
  • Gradually increase weights and focus on progressive overload.
  • Incorporate protein and carbohydrate-rich meals post-workout for recovery and muscle growth.
  • Stay hydrated and stretch regularly to improve muscle flexibility.
