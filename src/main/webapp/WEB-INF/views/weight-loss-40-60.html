Weight Loss Plan (40-65)

Diet Plan

  • Breakfast: Greek yogurt with chia seeds and sliced bananas.
  • Lunch: Grilled fish with mixed greens and quinoa.
  • Snacks: A handful of almonds and a protein shake.
  • Dinner: Baked turkey breast with roasted vegetables and a side salad.

Gym Routine

  • Low-impact cardio (walking, swimming, cycling) 4-5 days a week.
  • Strength training using lighter weights 2-3 days a week.
  • Focus on bodyweight exercises (squats, lunges, modified push-ups).
  • Incorporate yoga or stretching sessions for flexibility and balance.
