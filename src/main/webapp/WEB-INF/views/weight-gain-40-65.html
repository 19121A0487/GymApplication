Weight Gain Plan (40-65)

Diet Plan

  • Breakfast: Whole wheat toast with almond butter, 2 scrambled eggs, and a glass of full-fat milk.
  • Lunch: Turkey sandwich with whole grain bread, avocado, and mixed greens.
  • Snacks: Cottage cheese with fruit and a handful of almonds.
  • Dinner: Grilled chicken thighs, quinoa, and roasted vegetables.

Gym Routine

  • Low-impact strength training exercises (resistance bands, dumbbells) 3-4 days a week.
  • Focus on full-body workouts with lighter weights and higher repetitions.
  • Incorporate balance and flexibility exercises (yoga, Pilates) to improve joint mobility.
  • Ensure adequate rest and recovery to avoid injury.
